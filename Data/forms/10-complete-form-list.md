# Complete List of BC Supreme Court Family Rules Forms

## Source

This is the complete list of forms available under the Supreme Court Family Rules, as listed on the BC Government website at www2.gov.bc.ca under Justice > Courthouse Services > Documents & Forms > Court Forms > Supreme Court Family Rules forms. All forms are available as PDF downloads.

## Starting and Responding to a Case

- Form F1: Notice of Joint Family Claim — used when both parties agree to start a case together
- Form F1.1: Application for Divorce under the Civil Marriage Act (Canada)
- Form F1.2: Certificate of Divorce under the Civil Marriage Act (Canada)
- Form F2: Notice of Withdrawal from Joint Family Law Case — withdraw from a joint claim
- Form F3: Notice of Family Claim — the primary form to START a family law case (filed by the claimant)
- Form F4: Response to Family Claim — filed by the respondent within 30 days of being served
- Form F5: Counterclaim — filed by respondent WITH Form F4 when seeking additional orders
- Form F6: Response to Counterclaim — claimant's response to the respondent's counterclaim
- Form F7: Notice of Withdrawal in Family Law Case in Which a Divorce is Claimed

## Financial and Support Forms

- Form F8: Financial Statement — comprehensive disclosure of income, expenses, assets, debts. Required whenever support or property division is at issue
- Form F9: Agreement as to Annual Income — when parties agree on income amounts
- Form F37: Child Support Affidavit — specific information for calculating child support under the Federal Child Support Guidelines
- Form F102: Statement of Information for Corollary Relief Proceedings

## Applications and Responses

- Form F31: Notice of Application — ask the court for specific orders during a case (most common application form)
- Form F32: Application Response — respond to a Form F31 application
- Form F32.1: Order Signing Instructions
- Form F32.2: Cover Page
- Form F32.001: Requisition - Chambers Practice

## Affidavits and Evidence

- Form F30: Affidavit — general sworn statement used as evidence
- Form F38: Affidavit - Desk Order Divorce — specific affidavit for uncontested divorce
- Form F101: Affidavit - Section 51 — for child protection record checks
- Form F15: Affidavit of Personal Service — proof that documents were hand-delivered
- Form F16: Affidavit of Ordinary Service — proof of service by other methods (mail, email)
- Form F18: Certificate of Service by Sheriff

## Orders

- Form F33: Consent Order — when both parties agree on the terms
- Form F34: Order Made Without Notice — orders granted without the other party being present
- Form F51: Order Made After Application — standard order form after a hearing
- Form F51.1: Order Made at Judicial Case Conference
- Form F51.2: Order Made at Trial Management Conference
- Form F52: Final Order — the order made at trial or at the conclusion of the case
- Form F54: Protection Order — protects against family violence
- Form F54.1: Order Terminating a Protection Order
- Form F54.2: Restraining Order
- Form F83: Order to Register Foreign Judgment
- Form F85: Order to Waive Fees
